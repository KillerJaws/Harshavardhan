About Smart Hospital

This is my final year project. This includes doctor selection, Appointment booking and notification. The Upcoming version will have other special features like chat-bot assistance and User tracking, and e-commerce store


Problem Solved

These days it is not safe to gather in public places. The main problem we all experienced is stading in a queue for hours waiting for your turn. With this application you can book your appointment without stading in a queue and you can go to he hospital when it is your turn Even though you missed it You can re-schedule it from the same application.
